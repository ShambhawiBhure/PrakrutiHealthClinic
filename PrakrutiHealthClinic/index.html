Why Ayurveda?

Panchakarma Chikitsa

Panchakarma (meaning five karma) is the best medical method of Ayurveda . Panchakarma is called the unique medical system of Ayurveda. In many of these processes, five actions are the main ones, which is why it is called "Panchakarma". The processes of these five actions are as follows:

  • Vaman
  • Virechan
  • Basti - Anuvasan
  • Basti - Aasthapan
  • Nasya

Panchabhautik Chikitsa

The word panchamahabhuta is made up of three words: ‘pancha’, ‘maha’ and ‘bhuta’. ‘Pancha’ means five, ‘maha’ means great and ‘bhuta’ means that which exists. Panchabhautik chikitsa is a unique branch of Ayurveda. It is based on one of Ayurveda's core philosophies - macrocosm and microcosm. The five fundamental elements are:

  • Akasha mahabhuta
  • Vayu mahabhuta
  • Agni mahabhuta
  • Jala mahabhuta
  • Prithvi mahabhuta
